Boarding Horses

Skymeadow Farm in Greentown, PA is under new ownership and offers full horse boarding for just $300 a month. We will treat your horse as if he or she were our own. Quality feed/hay, and routine deworming is included. 34+ acres of pasture, wooded trails, heated tack room, indoor riding area, and stalls cleaned daily. We appeal to pleasure riders who LOVE animals. We do farm animal rescue and the horse boarding revenue helps us provide sanctuary/care for those animals, so... boarding with us makes a difference!
